My journey in the tech industry is fueled by a passion for innovation and solving complex problems in software development. While advancing my career, I'm expanding my expertise through Northeastern's Master of Science in Data Analytics and Engineering program (2025-2026), bringing together my diverse experiences in architecture, computer science, and data science to create impactful solutions.

Professional Background

My professional journey began with a Bachelor of Architecture from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, followed by a Master of Science in Design from the University of Pennsylvania. In May 2025, I completed my Master of Science in Computer Science at Northeastern University, where I engaged in research and practical projects that allowed me to apply my technical skills in real-world scenarios.

Now, as a founding software engineer at IDitor Inc, I'm building cutting-edge AI social applications that connect people in meaningful ways. My technical expertise spans a wide range of programming languages and frameworks, including Java, Python, TypeScript, JavaScript, Kotlin, C++, React.js, Next.js, and Node.js. My experience in architectural design has honed my abilities in project management, systems thinking, and creative problem-solving—skills that prove invaluable in startup environments where versatility and innovation are essential.
